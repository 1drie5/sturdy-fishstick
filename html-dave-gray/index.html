Mars — Quick Facts

Concise, scientifically accurate data about the planet Mars.

Mars at a glance
Property Value
Mean diameter 6,779 km (≈ 0.53 × Earth)
Mass 6.4171 × 10²³ kg (≈ 0.107 × Earth)
Surface gravity 3.71 m/s² (≈ 0.38 g)
Length of day 24 h 39 min (~1.03 Earth days)
Orbital period 687 Earth days (≈ 1.88 Earth years)
Average distance from Sun ≈ 227.9 million km (1.52 AU)
Moons 2 — Phobos and Deimos
Atmosphere (by volume) ~95.3% CO₂, 2.7% N₂, 1.6% Ar, traces of O₂ & CO
Surface pressure ≈ 610 Pa (≈ 0.006 atm; ~0.6% of Earth's)
Average surface temperature ≈−63 °C (range: ~−143 °C to +35 °C)
Notable features Olympus Mons (largest volcano), Valles Marineris (huge canyon), polar ice caps (H₂O & CO₂)
